## DebianDC Release Notes
<br>

---
### DebianDC Trixie
---
- Debian 13 (Trixie)
- LXDE Desktop -> Openbox
    - Openbox was adopted instead of the LXDE desktop environment
    - Scripts were made more modular and operation-focused
    - The initial-setup layer was added after ISO installation
    - The configuration process running at first boot was defined
    - The installation and runtime phases were clearly separated
